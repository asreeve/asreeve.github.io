About Me

Andrew S. Reeve is a professor in the School of Earth and Climate Sciences at the University of Maine. I pursue research activities related to groundwater hydrology and computer modeling, and using the Python Scripting language in my work. The courses I teach include introductory (First Year) Environmental Geology, Hydrogeology, and Python Scripting for Data Analysis. Other classes I have taught include Groundwater Modeling, Engineering Geology, and Fresh Water Flows (Introduction to Hydrology).

I am interested in opportunities to interpret environmental data sets or computer modeling related to hydrogeology and environmental geology topics.

For those interested, I am providing my Vitae and a shorter resume.
