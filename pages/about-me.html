About Me

Andrew Reeve is a professor in the School of Earth and Climate Sciences at the University of Maine. I pursue research activities related to groundwater hydrology, computer modeling, and using the Python Scripting language for Data Analysis. The courses I teach include introductory (First Year) Environmental Geology, Hydrogeology, and Python Scripting for Data Analysis.

In this web site, I will post information related to the classes I teach, research I am pursuing, and notes related to the things I am currently working on. I am interested in opportunities to interpret environmental data sets or computer modeling related to hydrogeology and environmental geology topics.

For those interested, I am providing my Vitae and a shorter resume.
