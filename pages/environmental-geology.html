Environmental Geology (ERS 102) covers how humans impact and are impacted by geologic processes. This includes geologic hazards (e.g. earthquakes), environmental pollution (e.g. groundwater contamination), and climate change. An old syllabus outlining the structure of this class and topics covered may be reviewed.
