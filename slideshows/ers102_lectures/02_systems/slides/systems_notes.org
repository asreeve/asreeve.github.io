* Feedback and Threshold
 * In the drainage from a basin, the feedback tends to regulate the level. 
 * this is a negative or balancing feedback
   * slows change in the system over time
   * what are examples of a negative feedback?
 * positive or reinforcing feedback
   * promotes change
   * acts as a snowballing effect...what does that mean?
 * Threshold 
   * sudden change in behavior when an amount passes a set level
   * acts as a tipping point in some cases...when the lake suddenly turns green and smelly from algae
* Feedback behavior
 * graphs of feedback plotted as time (x) vs. amount (y)
 * blue and green decrease slope over time, flatten; a negative feedback
 * teal line increases in slope over time, no sign of stopping, accelerating: positive feedback
